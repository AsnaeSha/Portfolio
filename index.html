Basket Analysis

In this project, I performed a Basket Analysis on 100 grocery store transactions to uncover product associations. The analysis revealed that 20% of transactions included both coffee and milk, with a 40% confidence of purchasing milk when coffee is bought and a lift value of 1.5, indicating a strong association. These insights can drive personalized recommendations, inventory optimization , and cross-selling strategies, such as placing avocados, tortilla chips, and salsa together to boost sales and enhance the shopping experience.
